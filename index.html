Chosen One

By Maury Guzman

The final war is approaching and all competitors are ready for it. Pablo and his younger brother, an underrated warrior, Matthew are prepared for their battle against Jacob, the greatest knight ever known. Whoever wins the war at the end will be respected, acknowledged, and rule the Kingdom they desire.

The throne and golden crown Pablo and Matthew battle Jacob for.

The brothers motivate each other for hope of a positive outcome of the war.

Jacob on the other hand, motivates his troopers to mentally prepare them for the bloodshed.

The two leaders of opposing tribes exchange words before the battle begins.

The war begins.

Pablo and Matthew lose the battle and negotiate a deal with jacob.

Jacob accepts the deal and now the greatest knight ever known and Pablo begin to fight.

Jacob stabs Pablo straight through his heart and Pablo drops dead.

Jacob accepts Matthew's battle request after killing his older brother Pablo.

Matthew moves his brother's body out the arena and replaces his sword with his brother's.

Jacob, the greatest knight ever known and Matthew, an underated warrior begin to battle till the death. Who ever wins this battle takes the throne.

Jacob slices Matthew's leg.

Matthew stands strong through the pain of his wound and continues fighting.

Matthew suddenly stabs Jacob in the throat and Jacob falls to the ground.

Matthew defeats Jacob but loses his brother in the process.

Matthew wears the golden crown and honors his brother one last time before paying his farewells.

Afterall matthew rules the kingdom but will never forget about his ilder brother Pablo.

A battle was lost, but the war was won.
